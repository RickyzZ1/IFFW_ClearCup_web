Life Around The Harvest

Coffee is the main cash crop for many homes here. What they earn in this season supports food, school costs, and household needs long after harvest ends.

Craft In The Washed Process

Teams monitor fermentation, clean-water washing, and drying-bed movement with strict routines. That discipline creates a cup with floral aromatics, citrus clarity, and clean structure.

Quality As Household Security

For producers, quality is not only pride. It is protection. Better prices mean fewer forced compromises at home and more confidence entering the next season.

"Every careful step at the station matters. Good quality is how we carry our families through the year."
