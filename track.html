Impact Tracking

What changed after trade: income, household life, and producer voices.

We track partner lots beyond transaction day. For each farmer group, we review trade outcomes, interview households, and log practical life changes. Visit cadence is set at every 6 months.

Review Cycle: Semiannual (every 6 months) Focus: Income fairness + quality continuity + household wellbeing
