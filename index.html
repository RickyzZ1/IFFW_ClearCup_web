About The ClearCup

Better coffee trade. Fairer farmer outcomes.

Who We Are

We are operators, not just advocates.

The ClearCup works with smallholder farmers, local cooperatives, importers, and ethical roasters. We turn fair-trade intent into daily execution through clearer lot information, cleaner transactions, and practical procurement support.

Our focus is simple: make it easier for responsible buyers to buy responsibly, and make it easier for small farmers to be paid fairly for great coffee.

Our Mission

Build inclusive import systems that work for the smallest farmers.

Use practical technology and collaborative trade design to expand market access, reduce risk exposure, and improve value capture for producer communities.

Our Vision

A specialty market where participation is defined by potential, not by size or power.

A coffee trade where small farmers are not excluded by scale or information gaps, and where value, quality, and long-term stability are shared more fairly across the chain.
